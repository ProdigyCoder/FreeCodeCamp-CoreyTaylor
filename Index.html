Corey Taylor

Lead singer of Slipknot and Stone Sour

Taylor at Comic Con '13 "Evil Dead" photoshoot.

Key historical events in Taylor's career:

  • 1973 - Taylor was born in Des Moines Iowa on December 8th.
  • 1992 - Corey and a few other memebers formed the hard rock band, "Stone Sour".
  • 1997 - Corey fills the vocalist replacement position in the rising heavy metal band Slipknot.
    • Slipknot in 1997
  • 1999 - Taylor releases his first album with Slipknot.
  • 2002 - Stone Sour releases their first album.
    • Stone Sour's self-titled album cover
  • 2005 - Taylor and Slipknot decided to take a break to focus on other musical careers. Taylor decided to focus mainly on Stone Sour
  • 2008 - Corey and Slipknot made a strong comeback with their 4th studio album All Hope is Gone
    • Slipknot during the All Hope is Gone era
  • 2010 - Corey's best friend and Slipknot's bass memeber Paul Gray past away resulting in a depressing time for Taylor, the band, and fans worldwide.
  • 2011 - Taylor published his first Autobiography titled "Seven Deadly Sins"
  • 2013 - Taylors wins best vocalist award in the The Revolver Golden Gods Awards
    • Taylor performing at the Golden Gods Awards
  • 2014 - Corey and Slipknot, once again, made a strong comeback with their 5th studio album and first album without Paul Gray titled .5 The Gray Chapter
